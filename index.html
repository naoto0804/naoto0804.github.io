Naoto Inoue

Hi, I am Naoto Inoue (井上 直人).

I am working as a Research Scientist at CyberAgent Inc. AILab.

Previously, I got my Ph.D. at The University of Tokyo in CV/CG under supervision of Prof. Toshihiko Yamasaki. My project was on developing novel methods and applications of generative modeling for visual content creation, especially using image-to-image translation. I was fortunate to collaborate with Adobe Research and Preferred Networks during my Ph.D. study.
